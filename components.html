USER'S GUIDELINES

Hope to help users better understand how to use this virtual circuit board to verify and debug circuit error

To Locate Every Other Component Apart From Wire And Voltage Source Onto The Breadboard

In order to place the other components such as resistor or the opamp as shown on the left, select the hole first the click Done button. For instance, if the hole A2 is selected then that would be the ltop leftmost node of the corresponding component (Which in this case would be that of resistor and opamp as shown).

How to place wire and voltage source onto the breadboard

Step (1) : select the starting hole
Step (2) : select the end hole
Step (3) : click 'Done' button
For instance, wire stretched from hole A1 and ends at C6
